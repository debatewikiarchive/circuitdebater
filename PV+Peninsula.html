http://paperlessdebate.com/verbatim/_layouts/15/start.aspx#/

I also added some more files from last year's topics with unbroken frontlines that we didn't post last year. I thought some of them might be useful, and it also might be useful for some people to see how our prep changed over the course of 2 years.

Hopefully, people will use the cases and frontlines to learn rather than to pilfer cards from them. I also decided to post full files with all the frontlines and Work In Progress sections so people could see our process and whatnot.
